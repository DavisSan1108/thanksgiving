What is Thanksgiving?

Thanksgiving is a time to give thanks for the harvest and blessings of the past year. It is celebrated in the United States on the fourth Thursday of November.

Traditional Foods

  • Turkey
  • Mashed Potatoes
  • Stuffing
  • Cranberry Sauce
  • Pumpkin Pie

Fun Fact

Did you know? The first Thanksgiving was celebrated in 1621 by the Pilgrims and the Native Americans.
